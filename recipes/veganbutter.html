Vegan Butter

Description

This recipe is a little expensive, but still has a lower price if you consider commertial vegan butters.

Ingredients

    1 cup coconut oil
    2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
    1/4 cup olive oil
    1/2 cup plant-based milk
    Tumeric to taste
    salt to taste

Steps

    Place all ingredients on a bowl
    Mix everything
    Try it to see if there is enough salt
    Place it on a pot and put in the fridge for at least 2 hours
